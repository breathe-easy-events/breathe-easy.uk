Breathe Easy Sheffield

💃 Do you enjoy going out and socialising, but feel cautious since the covid-19 pandemic? Are you avoiding crowded and indoor venues? You are not alone!

😷 Many people are still worried about the risks of attending in-person events, especially people who are clinically vulnerable to covid-19 and other infectious diseases.

🌤️ Breathe Easy Sheffield is a new eclectic series of social and cultural events, designed with enhanced safety measures in place to reduce transmission risk. Launching autumn 2024.
